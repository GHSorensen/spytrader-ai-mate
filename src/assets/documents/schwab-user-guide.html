Getting Started with SPY Trading AI and Your Schwab Account

This guide will walk you through the process of connecting your Charles Schwab account to SPY Trading AI for paper trading or live trading.

Note: SPY Trading AI uses secure OAuth2 authentication to connect to your Schwab account without storing your credentials.

Step 1: Selecting the Right Account Type

Before connecting to SPY Trading AI, make sure you have the appropriate Schwab account:

  • For Paper Trading: Any Schwab brokerage account can be used for paper trading, as this doesn't involve real money.
  • For Live Trading: You need a Schwab brokerage account with options trading permissions to trade SPY options.

If you don't have options trading permissions, you will need to apply for them through Schwab's website or by contacting Schwab customer service.

Step 2: Connecting Your Schwab Account

  1. On the SPY Trading AI dashboard, click the "Settings" icon in the top-right corner.
  2. In the settings dialog, select the "Broker" tab.
  3. From the broker dropdown, select "Charles Schwab".
  4. Click the "Connect to Schwab" button.
  5. You will be redirected to Schwab's login page. Enter your Schwab credentials.
  6. After logging in, Schwab will ask you to authorize SPY Trading AI to access your account. Review the permissions and click "Authorize".
  7. You will be redirected back to SPY Trading AI with a confirmation message.

Step 3: Selecting an Account

If you have multiple Schwab accounts:

  1. After connecting, you'll see a dropdown of all available accounts.
  2. Select the specific account you want to use with SPY Trading AI.
  3. Click "Confirm Selection" to proceed.

Step 4: Choose Paper or Live Trading

  1. After selecting your account, you'll be prompted to choose between Paper Trading and Live Trading.
  2. For beginners or testing, we strongly recommend starting with Paper Trading.
  3. Select your preferred trading mode and click "Confirm".
Important: Live Trading involves real money and real risk. Only choose this option if you fully understand the risks involved and are prepared for potential losses.

Step 5: Verify Connection

  1. After completing the setup, you'll see a "Connection Status" indicator showing "Connected to Schwab".
  2. The system will display your account balance and current positions (if any).
  3. Verify that this information is correct before proceeding.

Step 6: Start Trading SPY

You're now ready to use SPY Trading AI with your Schwab account:

  • For Paper Trading: The system will simulate trades without using real money.
  • For Live Trading: The AI will execute real trades based on its analysis and your settings.

All trades will be focused on SPY (S&P 500 ETF) options.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Connection Failed: If connection fails, try again and make sure you're entering the correct Schwab credentials.
  • Account Not Listed: Only accounts with appropriate permissions will be displayed. Contact Schwab if you believe an account is missing.
  • Permission Errors: Make sure your Schwab account has the necessary permissions for options trading.
  • Session Expired: You may occasionally need to reconnect your account if your session expires. This is normal and part of Schwab's security measures.

Support Resources

If you encounter issues connecting your Schwab account:

  • Check our FAQ section for common problems and solutions
  • Contact our support team at support@spytradingai.com
  • For Schwab-specific issues, contact Schwab customer service at 1-800-435-4000

Security Note: SPY Trading AI never stores your Schwab password. We use OAuth2 authentication, which means your credentials are only shared with Schwab, not with our application.
